About Frogs

A frog is any member of a diverse and largely carnivorous group of short-bodied, tailless amphibians composing the order Anura. Research suggests thier split from amphibians may extend all the way back to the Permian Geolocial period, 256 million ears ago.

Fun Frog Facts

  • There are over 7,600 species of frogs.
  • Frogs make up 88% of amphibian species.
  • Frogs are considered the best jumpers of all vertebrates relative to their size.
